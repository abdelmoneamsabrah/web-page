FAQ

  • Why did you launch a new site?

    You asked, we listened and couldn't wait to bring all your suggestions to life. So we did, and the new site was born. We're making it easier for you to plan trips, contribute content and find exactly what you're looking for. (We tried launching a space shuttle too, but let's just say it was a bit too complicated for us web folks).

  • What has changed?

    To make it easier for you to find the most helpful, relevant advice, we did some reorganizing, took on a new look and changed the names of some things.

  • Did my account change too?

    Your account information did not change, and you will still receive all the newsletters you're currently signed up for. Any contributions you've made to TripAdvisor will be there too, just as uncensored and honest as you remember them.

  • Why have only some pages been redesigned?

    We're rolling the site out gradually so we can incorporate your feedback and ideas on every page, every step of the way. After all, Rome wasn't built in a day – and that's a good thing. (Can you imagine what the Coliseum would look like?).

  • What are the payment methods?

    Payment method is by credit card only.

  • So it's easier to write reviews now, right?

    How did you know? Now there are more pages with callouts for you to post your own hotel, restaurant and attraction reviews – and those photos and videos you're so good at taking. Just between us, we hope you post often. We're dying to hear your opinions and those priceless "I can't believe it really happened" stories.

  • What about any promotions I entered?

    If you entered a promotion on the old site, you're still in the running and eligible to win. We're always putting up new sweepstakes and giving stuff away, so make sure the contest hound in you is ready and on the lookout.

  • How can I give you my feedback and suggestions?

    Just shout through your megaphone and we'll hear you. On second thought, you can share your thoughts with us here. Please tell us anything and everything. We take your comments seriously and use them to help make TripAdvisor a better, more helpful site for you.
